Waikiki Beach

Relax on the world-famous Waikiki Beach, known for its golden sands and turquoise waters.

Volcanoes National Park

Witness the power of nature with active volcanoes, lava fields, and stunning landscapes.

Haleakala Sunrise

Catch a breathtaking sunrise at the summit of Haleakala, Maui's highest peak.

Activities to Try

  • Snorkeling and scuba diving in pristine coral reefs
  • Hiking through lush rainforests and waterfalls
  • Exploring Hawaiian cultural sites and luaus
  • Surfing lessons on some of the best waves in the world
  • Helicopter tours for aerial views of the islands

Travel Tips

  1. Respect local culture and traditions, including the practice of leaving rocks or sand behind.
  2. Pack reef-safe sunscreen to protect marine life and coral reefs.
  3. Book activities and accommodations in advance, especially during peak seasons.
  4. Rent a car to explore beyond the main tourist areas.
  5. Learn a few Hawaiian phrases, such as "Aloha" (hello/love) and "Mahalo" (thank you).
